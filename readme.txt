MyMovieMatrix is a One Stop discovery platform for Indian Movies & TV Shows. 

== Description ==

MyMovieMatrix is a One Stop discovery platform for Indian Movies & TV Shows. It enables discovery of Indian Content, easy Identification of personalized choice with our automated recommendation and Sources to consume it from anytime anywhere. 

The Widget helps you discover Movies & TV Shows across 5 Languages - Hindi, English, Tamil, Telugu & Kannada.
The plugin will show all the Latest Releases and Trending picks of Movies, TV Shows in India and suggests similar content to watch. 

Users will get the reviews and ratings for the content shown in the plugin. The plugin also extends the recommendation to other categories like Games & Books. 

It is the entertainment quotient for your Blog and Website. Adds a great value add of information on Indian Movies & TV Shows to your users.  

= Features =
1. The plugin gathers all the latest information - Movie Posters, Ratings, and Reviews from different critic sites. 
2. It also shows the link to watch the content or book any ticket for the User. 
3. MyMovieMatrix recommends similar content within the language and also across the Languages. 
4. The content is shown for:
    * New Releases 
    * Movies on TV 
    * Popular Picks 
    * Categories 
    * TV Series 
    * Related Games & Books 
5. Content info will have links on:
    * Streaming Sources to watch immediately. 
    * Ticket Booking for running movies. 
    * TV Channel and Time Guide. 
    * Purchase Links 
    * Reviews from multiple sites. 
6. Categories of content:
    * Award Nominees 
    * Top Lists 
    * Song Videos 
    * Curated Content lists - Ex - Chic Flicks, War, Sword & Sorcery etc 

== Installation ==

1. Upload `mymoviematrix.php` to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory
1. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
1. Place `<?php do_action('mymoviematrix'); ?>` in your templates
